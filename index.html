Assignment 8: Javascript Charts

by Aaron Albright

The charts I chose to make are based on the US Census Bureau's population projections for the next 45 years. WHile it's a natural assumption that the US population will grow over time, I found it particularlly interesting to see the projections for the native-born versus foreign-born population. I then broke those numebrs down based on sex. While the change isn't drastic, there is a significant increase in foreign-born men and women. Both sexes are projected to double. As a result, the foreign-born population will hold a higher perctage of the total US population. Meanwhile, the percentage difference between native-born men and women continues to narrow, approaching an even, 50-50 split.

Source: US Census Bureau: Table 2. Projections of the Population by Nativity for the United States: 2015 to 2060
